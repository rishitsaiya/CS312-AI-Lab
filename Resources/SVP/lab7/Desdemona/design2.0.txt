Desdemona - A Othello Competition Framework
===========================================

Desdemona is an Othello competition framework created to allow students to play their AI bots against each other. It is being created for the "CS698 - Introduction to Artificial Intelligence" course, taken at IIT Madras.

Features:
* Plug-in like functionality for each bot. Submitted bots are in a ".so" format.
    * Each bot must expose 2 functions:
        * createBot
        * destroyBot
* Save and playback games
* Console visualisation of the games (can be enabled)
